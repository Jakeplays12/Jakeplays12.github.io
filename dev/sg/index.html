    Skade Gaming is a free online gaming community for Autistics (14+) and family members.

    What Does Skade Gaming have to Offer?

    We currently offer 13 different family-friendly games, all rated T and under. One of our main games is Minecraft, and we provide access to three servers, Survival, Creative and Modded.

    How is Skade Gaming kept safe?

    The Skade Gaming community is monitored by qualified Administrators to assure a safe place for players to interact with each other without the fear of bullying or criticism.

    What is the goal of Skade Gaming?

    Our Goal is to provide a safe family-oriented environment for Autistics and family members of all social and economic statuses to participate in an interactive experience.
