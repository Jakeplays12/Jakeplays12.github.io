    Skade Rules:

  • 👎 Harassing or Bullying is Not Allowed
  • 📲 No Promoting alternative Servers
  • 📛 No Inappropriate Names, Skins, or Profile Pictures
  • 🚫 Religious, Sexual, or Political Talk is Not Allowed

  • 🥸 No Imitating other Skaders without consent (Includes Names, Profile Pictures, Skins, etc)
  • ☝ Don't Be That Guy: Don't be the bad example & treat others how you want to be treaded (AKA The Golden Rule)
