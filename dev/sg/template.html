    Skader Of The Month:

    This month’s Skader of the month is MarioGerms (Evan)! Congratulations to them!

    End Spawn Team

    September 2, 2023, we will be having a meeting for our next build team that will work on planning and building our next big build project on the Midgard Survival Minecraft server the End Spawn! If you are interested, please make sure you are at this meeting.

    Community-Wide Meeting

    September 23, 2023 at 3pm EST, we will be having a community-wide meeting to go over things in the community and to allow us to just check in on the community and maybe make some changes to things that other skaders might have input on changing. Everyone definitely should try to be there look through the discord, minecraft servers and other things to see what kind of input you might want to bring to the community.

    Asgard 2.0

    We are getting about ready to completely redo our Modded Minecraft server Asgard. Getting it on a new version with all new mods. Just like before, you can suggest what versions and modpacks you might like to have for the server. We are looking at a version not below 1.16.4 and not above 1.18.2. Keep that in mind when you suggest your modpacks, and if someone suggests a modpack that maybe you don’t like or you know it may not work, just leave it, it is that individual’s suggestions same rules apply like in the ideas forums.

    Pack Team Shop

    There is now a new shop in the Mall of Vanir on the Midgard Minecraft server at the Air Hall, the Pack Team shop. This showcases all of the custom items that are on the resource pack for the server that you can make on your own. This is just a place where you can go to see everything that you can make yourself with the pack.
